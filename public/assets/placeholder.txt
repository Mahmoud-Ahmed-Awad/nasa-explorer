## Recommended Asset Replacements

### 3D Models
- **rocket.glb**: Place a 3D rocket model here
  - Recommended sources: NASA 3D models, Sketchfab (CC licensed)
  - Format: GLTF/GLB
  - Size: Keep under 5MB for web performance

### Image Specifications

#### Mission Images
- **Size**: 800x600px minimum
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9

#### Satellite Images
- **Size**: 400x300px minimum
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3

#### Team Photos
- **Size**: 300x300px (square)
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Style**: Professional headshots

### Usage Notes

1. **Licensing**: Ensure all images are properly licensed for commercial use
2. **Performance**: Optimize images for web (compress, use WebP when possible)
3. **Accessibility**: Include alt text for all images
4. **Responsive**: Consider providing multiple sizes for different screen densities
